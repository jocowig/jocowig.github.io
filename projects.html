Collaboration Station

Collaboration Station is the name of an Android game made as an immersive learning project with a multidisciplinary team to teach children about the International Space Station. I was a programmer with a team of ten students (two artists, one music major, one literature major and seven programmers) which also worked together with the Children's Museum in Indianapolis.

Robot Turtles

For a nine week project I worked on with a team for an advanced programming class, we decided to make an android adaptation of a popular board game called Robot Turtles. The board game is designed to introduce young children to abstract thinking which is the core of programming. The game requires the player to enter a series of commands for the turtle to follow in order to reach a jewel.

IU Interactive

Summer 2015 I was given the opportunity to participate in an Oculus Rift Hackathon at IU. The challenge was to design an interactive virtual reality experience for the Oculus Rift using Unity displaying the IU campus to potential students and athletes. For our work we were awarded with an Oculus DK2 and Leap Motion each. The whole project is a little too big to upload here, but I have copies available if anyone is interested.
